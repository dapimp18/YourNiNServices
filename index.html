Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Information We Collect

When you use our NIN enrolment service, we collect the following personal information about your child and family as required by the National Identity Management Commission (NIMC):

  • Child's full name, date of birth, and gender
  • State of origin and local government area
  • Residential address and phone number
  • Parents' full names and National Identification Number (NIN)
  • Child's photograph

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for the purpose of enrolling your child in Nigeria's National Identity Database via the NIMC portal. We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties outside of the official NIMC enrolment process.

3. Data Retention

We retain your submitted information only for as long as necessary to complete the NIN enrolment and provide follow-up support. Once the enrolment is confirmed, data is securely deleted within 90 days.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, alteration, or destruction. Form submissions are transmitted over encrypted connections.

5. Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to access, correct, or request deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@yournin.services.

6. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at hello@yournin.services.
